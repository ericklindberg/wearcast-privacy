WearCast Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 29, 2026

1. Information We Collect

Location Data: WearCast uses your device's location to provide weather-based outfit recommendations. Location data is processed locally on your device and transmitted to our weather API partner (Open-Meteo) to fetch relevant weather data. We do not store your precise GPS coordinates on our servers.

Weather Data: We collect weather information (temperature, humidity, conditions) associated with your approximate location to generate outfit recommendations.

Push Notification Preferences: If you enable notifications, we store your notification opt-in status securely.

Usage Information: We may collect anonymous, aggregated data about how the app is used to improve the service. This data cannot be used to identify you.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide weather-based outfit recommendations tailored to current conditions
  • To send push notifications about outfit reminders if you opt in
  • To improve and maintain the WearCast service
  • To comply with legal obligations

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption. Location data is only retained temporarily for the purpose of generating recommendations and is not linked to your identity. You can request deletion of any stored data at any time by contacting us.

4. Third-Party Services

Open-Meteo: Weather data is provided by Open-Meteo (open-meteo.com). Their privacy policy governs their data practices.

Expo / React Native: The app is built using Expo and React Native frameworks.

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

5. Push Notifications

WearCast may send push notifications to remind you to check your outfit recommendation. You can disable notifications at any time through your device settings or within the WearCast app settings.

6. Children's Privacy

WearCast is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. Contact us at lindberg.erick720@gmail.com.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Significant changes will be notified in the app.
